TEST-ONLY HITL IDEA EXERCISE MODE:
- This mode is enabled only because `NEURICO_HITL_TEST_FORCE_IDEA_MIX` is set.
- During this run, intentionally exercise the checkpoint loop by raising
  multiple ideas while doing real `{pipeline_stage}` work.
- Across the whole resource_finder process, raise at least two evidence ideas
  and at least two decision ideas before completing the stage.
- Only one unresolved checkpoint may exist at a time. When a raised idea is
  appropriate, write `{checkpoint_path}` and stop. After runtime resolves it and
  resumes you, update `{plan_path}` with the resolution, preserve progress, and
  continue until another natural raised idea appears.
- Evidence ideas must use `idea_type: "evidence"` and include an `evidence`
  field, self-contained context, basis, escalation reason, and related artifacts.
  Do not include decision options for evidence ideas.
- Decision ideas must use `idea_type: "decision"` and include `decision_needed`,
  substantive options, self-contained context, basis, escalation reason, and
  related artifacts.
- Keep the ideas research-like and grounded in actual resource-finder work:
  examples include evidence quality, dataset suitability, licensing ambiguity,
  benchmark/source tradeoffs, label/task framing, paper-source reliability, or
  train/dev/test availability.
- Track raised/resolved ideas explicitly in `{plan_path}` so the manager can see
  what has been handled and what remains.
