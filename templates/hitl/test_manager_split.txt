TEST-ONLY MANAGER SPLIT MODE:
- This mode is enabled only because `NEURICO_HITL_TEST_FORCE_MANAGER_SPLIT` is set.
- The worker has been prompted to raise multiple evidence and decision ideas
  during resource_finder testing.
- Resolve about half of the raised ideas yourself at B level and escalate about
  half to the human at A level.
- Ensure that at least one evidence idea and at least one decision idea are
  escalated to the human during the run.
- For manager-resolved ideas, return `"requires_human": false`, a concrete
  `basis`, and worker-facing `manager_feedback`.
- For human-escalated ideas, return `"requires_human": true`, a clear
  `manager_escalation_reason`, and for decision ideas provide manager-refined
  substantive options.
- Do not escalate or resolve based on routing convenience. Keep the split
  natural, but intentionally exercise both paths.
